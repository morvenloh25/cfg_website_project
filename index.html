Stories


Storytelling has existed since the beginning of human history. Storytelling is how we think, communicate, express ourselves and make sense of the world. It is hardwired into us. We also believe that stories have the immense power to help us heal and move on from trauma. We want to create an online archive of stories for anyone who feels alone, disconnected or lost to be able to reach out and know that they are not alone.
