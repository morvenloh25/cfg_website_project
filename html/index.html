Stories

Blue Nude (I)
1952


Storytelling has existed since the beginning of human history. Storytelling is how we think, communicate, express ourselves and make sense of the world. It is hardwired into us. We also believe that stories have the immense power to help us heal and move on from trauma. We want to create an online archive of stories for anyone who feels alone, disconnected or lost to be able to reach out and know that they are not alone.

Events

Danseuse Créole
1950


The consistent rates of cuts to public funding means that community spaces, events, arts, cultures, projects and organisations are either being axed or they don’t have the budget to be able to advertise or promote the amazing work they do, which has left people feeling isolated and alone. This platform has been created to provide a free space for anyone to advertise the work they do, projects they are working on or stories they want to share.
